ABOUT DAVIDA'S WORK

AUTHOR

This self-help book is a powerful guide on acceptance, forgiveness, self-love and what it takes to overcome childhood adversities and trauma. Through applying biblical principles and using Q&A to create a written log of your past events, this book will help jumpstart your journey towards wholeness. It will challenge you and ultimately leave you wanting to do more toward self- healing and self-care. My belief is that it’s never too late to begin again.

ABOUT

Davida Thompson is a life coach who naturally gravitated toward women’s empowerment due to her personal experiences. Davida was also motivated by hurting women who struggled with managing work-life balance. Having survived molestation, mental abuse, domestic violence, and neglect, from that point forward it was her goal to help motivate women to live happy and healthier lives.
